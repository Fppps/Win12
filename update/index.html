Dynamic Taskbar

The taskbar is fully fragmented. Drag segments, combine them, and watch the corners mathematically auto-adjust.

Widget Combos

Liquid Glass & Mica Materials

Experience our new rendering engine. True background-filtering blurs the desktop behind UI elements, while Mica provides an elegant opaque texture to typography and surfaces.

Mastering the Desktop

Try these quick gestures to customize your workspace.

1

Combining Widgets

Drag an app from the start menu or an existing taskbar widget and drop it over the exact center of another taskbar widget to merge them into one glass panel.

2

Extracting & Unpinning

Click and drag an individual app icon out of a taskbar container and drop it onto an empty space on the taskbar to separate it, or drop it onto the Start menu to unpin.

3

Reorganizing the Taskbar

Click and hold on an empty space within a taskbar segment to drag it left or right to sort it between other active segments on your screen.
