Welcome to 1H05 Preview

This new update introduces a stunning zero shadow desktop concept featuring pure glassy reflections and highly saturated mica blur effects.

  • Advanced Personalization: Complete control over your taskbar appearance through live transparency adjustments and custom color palette selections.

Experience Windows 12

Build 1H05 is here. Let's see what's changed.

Dynamic Taskbar

The taskbar is fully fragmented. Drag segments, combine them, and watch the corners mathematically auto-adjust.

Widget Combos

Drag any app directly from the Start Menu onto the taskbar, or drop it ON an existing widget to merge them.

Liquid Glass & Mica Materials

Experience our new rendering engine. True background-filtering blurs the desktop behind UI elements, while Mica provides an elegant opaque texture.

Mastering the Desktop

Try these quick gestures to customize your workspace.

1

Combining Widgets

Drag an app from the start menu or an existing taskbar widget and drop it over the exact center of another taskbar widget to merge them.

2

Extracting & Unpinning

Click and drag an individual app icon out of a taskbar container and drop it onto an empty space on the taskbar to separate it.

3

Reorganizing the Taskbar

Click and hold on an empty space within a taskbar segment to drag it left or right to sort it between other active segments.
